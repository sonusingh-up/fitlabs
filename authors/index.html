How Every Review Is Produced

Every product goes through the same 5-step process before a FitLab Score is assigned. Scores are weighted across five criteria — no shortcuts, no exceptions.

Step 01
Ingredient Analysis
30% of score
Every ingredient checked against peer-reviewed research for efficacy, bioavailability, and safety at the dose used.
Step 02
Dosing Accuracy
25% of score
Each dose compared against clinically effective amounts. Underdosed ingredients are flagged and penalised in the score.
Step 03
Label Transparency
20% of score
Proprietary blends lose points. Full ingredient and dose disclosure — plus third-party certifications — is the standard.
Step 04
Real-World Results
15% of score
Taste, mixability, and actual effect over 4–8 weeks factored in where products are personally tested.
Step 05
Value for Money
10% of score
Cost per effective dose calculated and compared to alternatives. Premium pricing without premium formula is penalised.
