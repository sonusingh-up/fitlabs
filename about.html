Our Mission

Honest Reviews in an Industry Full of Hype

The supplement industry generates over $150 billion annually — and most of that is fueled by marketing, not science. Prop blends hide underdosed ingredients. Flashy ads make ordinary products sound revolutionary. Review sites rank products based on affiliate payout, not efficacy.

FitLabReviews exists to cut through that noise. My goal is simple: help you make better purchasing decisions with your health and money.

When I recommend a product, I do so because I believe it genuinely delivers on its claims — not because the brand is paying me a high commission rate. When a product doesn't live up to the hype, I say that clearly, even if it costs me revenue.

How We Review

Our Methodology

Every supplement goes through a structured 5-step review process before a score is assigned.

1
Ingredient Analysis

We analyze every ingredient against current peer-reviewed literature, checking for evidence of efficacy, bioavailability, and safety at the doses used.

2
Dosing Accuracy Check

We compare serving sizes and dosages against the clinically effective doses established in research. Underdosed "fairy dust" ingredients are flagged and penalized.

3
Label Transparency Audit

Proprietary blends lose points. Products with full label disclosure — every ingredient, every dose — are scored higher. We also check for third-party testing certifications (NSF, Informed Sport, etc.).

4
Real-World Testing

Where possible, we test the product personally or gather feedback from verified users. Taste, mixability, and actual performance over 4–8 weeks are factored into the score.

5
Value Assessment

We calculate the cost per effective dose and compare it to alternatives. A great formula that costs 3x its competitors will have points deducted from its final score.
