Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2025 · Effective date: March 1, 2025

📋 Summary: FitLabReviews.com collects limited data to operate the site and improve your experience. We use Google Analytics for traffic insights, affiliate links that may set cookies, and an email newsletter you can unsubscribe from at any time. We do not sell your personal data.

1. Who We Are

FitLabReviews.com ("we", "us", "our") is an independent supplement review and fitness blog operated by Jake Reynolds. We are based in the United States. You can contact us at: contact@fitlabreview.com

2. What Data We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use our website:

  • Usage data: Pages visited, time on site, browser type, IP address (anonymized), referring URL — collected automatically via Google Analytics.
  • Email address: Only if you voluntarily subscribe to our newsletter. We never add you without your explicit consent.
  • Cookies: We use cookies for analytics (Google Analytics) and affiliate tracking. See Section 5 for full cookie details.
  • Contact form submissions: Your name and email if you submit a contact form. This data is used solely to respond to your message.

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any sensitive personal data.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect for the following purposes:

  • To understand how visitors use our site and improve content quality (Google Analytics)
  • To send you our email newsletter if you subscribed (you can unsubscribe any time)
  • To respond to messages or inquiries sent via our contact form
  • To track affiliate link clicks and earn commissions from our affiliate partners (Clickbank, MaxWeb, GuruMedia, Amazon Associates)

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

4. Affiliate Disclosure & Tracking

FitLabReviews.com participates in affiliate marketing programs. When you click an affiliate link on our site and make a purchase, we may earn a commission — at no additional cost to you. Our affiliate partners include:

  • Clickbank — digital health and fitness products
  • MaxWeb — health and nutraceutical offers
  • GuruMedia — supplement and fitness offers
  • Amazon Associates — physical supplement products

These affiliate networks may set cookies on your device to track that a referral came from our site. These cookies typically expire within 30–90 days. This tracking enables us to receive commissions for qualifying purchases. Please review the privacy policies of each respective network for more details on how they handle data.

Important: Affiliate relationships never influence our product scores or recommendations. We recommend products because they are genuinely good, not because they pay higher commissions.

5. Cookies

We use the following cookies on FitLabReviews.com:

  • Google Analytics cookies (_ga, _gid, _gat): Used to track page views, session duration, and traffic sources. Data is anonymized. Google's Privacy Policy →
  • Affiliate tracking cookies: Set by our affiliate networks when you click a product link. These track whether a purchase was referred from our site.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time. Note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of some features. To opt out of Google Analytics tracking specifically, use the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect data independently:

  • Google Analytics (analytics.google.com) — website traffic analytics
  • Google Fonts — font delivery (may log request metadata)
  • Email newsletter provider — stores subscriber email addresses

Each of these services operates under its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review them if you have concerns.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request that we correct inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your personal data
  • Unsubscribe: Opt out of our email newsletter at any time via the unsubscribe link in any email
  • GDPR (EU residents): You have all rights under the General Data Protection Regulation
  • CCPA (California residents): You have the right to know what data is collected and to opt out of data sales (we do not sell data)

To exercise any of these rights, email us at contact@fitlabreview.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy

FitLabReviews.com is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted personal data to us, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Data Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect any data we hold. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure. We encourage you to use caution when sharing personal information online.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the site after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us:

  • Email: contact@fitlabreview.com
  • Website: fitlabreview.com/about
