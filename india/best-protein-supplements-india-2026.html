🇮🇳 India Supplement Market — What You Must Know Before Buying

Amino-spiking is rampant in India: Cheap amino acids (taurine, glycine, creatine) are added to inflate protein content on labels. Brands like MuscleBlaze, ON, and AS-IT-IS are clean. Always verify protein per 100g — a genuine whey should show 70-85% protein by weight on the label.

Price reality after import duty: International proteins (ON, Dymatize, Isopure) are 40-70% more expensive in India than the US price due to import duties and GST. Budget ₹3,500-5,500/kg for international brands vs ₹1,200-2,800/kg for quality domestic brands.

Amazon India authenticity: Buy only from the official brand store or verified sellers with 1,000+ reviews. Third-party sellers at suspicious discounts on Amazon India have been flagged for counterfeit products, especially for ON and Dymatize.

📖 India Protein Buyer's Guide

How to Spot Amino-Spiked Protein in India

Amino-spiking inflates protein readings with cheap non-essential amino acids (taurine, glycine, creatine). Warning signs: protein % per 100g below 65%, unusually cheap price for "25g protein", presence of taurine or glycine high in the ingredients list. Always check the protein-per-100g figure, not just the headline "per serving" claim.

Frequently Asked Questions
Which is the best protein supplement in India for beginners?
MuscleBlaze Biozyme Performance Whey is the best starting point for most Indian beginners — FSSAI certified, proven anti-amino-spiking, EAF enzyme complex for better digestion (helpful since ~60% of South Asians have some degree of lactase insufficiency), and priced competitively at ₹2,799/kg. AS-IT-IS WPC is the best budget option at ₹1,299/kg if you don't mind unflavoured.
Is ON Gold Standard Whey worth the price in India?
For users who specifically need Informed Sport certification (competitive athletes), or who want the world's most studied whey protein formula — yes. For general fitness use, MuscleBlaze Biozyme delivers 90% of the same outcome at 80% of the cost in India. The ₹700/kg premium buys certification, not meaningfully better muscle protein synthesis outcomes.
How do I avoid fake supplements on Amazon India?
Buy only from the official brand store (check the 'Ships from and sold by' field — it should say the brand or a verified distributor, not an unknown third party). Check seller ratings — avoid anyone under 90% positive with fewer than 500 reviews selling branded protein. Verify the product's hologram or QR code against the brand's official verification system. Both MuscleBlaze and ON have mobile verification tools.
Is whey protein allowed in India? Is it FSSAI regulated?
Yes — whey protein is classified as a food supplement under FSSAI regulations and is fully legal for sale and consumption in India. FSSAI certification means the product has met basic food safety standards for the Indian market. Domestic brands like MuscleBlaze and AS-IT-IS carry FSSAI certification; international brands sold through import typically do not require separate FSSAI certification under the Foreign Direct Investment rules.
What is amino-spiking and which Indian brands are clean?
Amino-spiking adds cheap amino acids (taurine, glycine, creatine) to a protein blend to inflate the nitrogen content that shows up as 'protein' on standard protein tests (Kjeldahl method). Labs that test for specific amino acid profiles will detect it. Clean brands in India include: MuscleBlaze (Biozyme range), AS-IT-IS, ON Gold Standard, and Dymatize. Brands with past spiking allegations include several private-label supplements sold through small retailers — avoid unbranded or heavily discounted unknown brands.
