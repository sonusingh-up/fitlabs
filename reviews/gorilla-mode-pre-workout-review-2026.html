What Is Gorilla Mode Pre-Workout?

Gorilla Mode was created by Derek from MorePlatesMoreDates — the YouTube bodybuilding and supplement science channel with 1.5M+ subscribers. It is not a celebrity brand in the typical sense: Derek's content is genuinely science-focused and he created Gorilla Mode specifically to address what he considered a market-wide problem of pre-workout under-dosing. The formula's centrepiece is the highest pure L-Citrulline dose in any mainstream pre-workout at 9g per 2 scoops — significantly above the 6g ceiling that most premium competitors use even in their clinical claims.

The current formula (V2) represents a refined version that dropped the controversial Eria Jarensis (N-Phenethyl Dimethylamine Citrate) from the original and replaced it with Kanna (Sceletium tortuosum) as the mood-modulating component — a more novel but less stimulant-like ingredient. The result is a formula designed around pump first, focus second, and clean energy third.

📚 Why 9g of pure citrulline matters: The clinical literature on citrulline uses doses ranging from 3g to 9g. Pérez-Guisado & Jakeman (2010) used 8g citrulline malate (equivalent to ~5.3g pure citrulline). Gorilla Mode's 9g of pure citrulline provides significantly more active citrulline than any citrulline malate product at the same gram weight — because citrulline malate 2:1 contains only 67% citrulline by weight. This is a meaningful quality and quantity advantage.

Key Ingredient Deep-Dive

L-Citrulline — 9,000mg Pure (2 scoops)
Max clinical dose
Pure — Not Malate 2:1IOC Category AMaximum clinical dose

Pure L-Citrulline (not citrulline malate) means the entire 9,000mg is active citrulline with no malic acid dilution. At this dose, nitric oxide production, vasodilation, and the visual pump effect are consistently at their maximum attainable level from supplementation alone. This single feature is Gorilla Mode's defining differentiator. See our citrulline science guide.

Creatine Monohydrate — 5,000mg (2 scoops)
Full 5g clinical dose
ISSN Clinical Dose ✔Rare in Pre-Workouts

A full 5g creatine dose in a pre-workout is genuinely rare — most skip it entirely or add 1–2g as window dressing. Gorilla Mode users are getting both their pre-workout and their creatine simultaneously, which is a meaningful practical and cost advantage. Long-term users will reach full muscle phosphocreatine saturation without separate creatine supplementation.

Kanna (Sceletium tortuosum) — 500mg
Mood modulator
Serotonin Reuptake InhibitorUnique IngredientLimited Human RCT Data

Kanna is a South African succulent used traditionally for mood elevation and anxiety reduction. Pharmacologically, it acts as a selective serotonin reuptake inhibitor (SSRI) and PDE4 inhibitor at the doses used in supplements. The 500mg dose in Gorilla Mode is designed to "take the edge off" the high-caffeine stimulant load — reducing anxiety and enhancing calm focus without dulling alertness. Novel ingredient with growing evidence base; not yet at the same evidence tier as caffeine or citrulline.

Who Should Buy Gorilla Mode

✅Buy if you are: An experienced lifter who prioritises the muscle pump experience above all else — the 9g pure citrulline + 3g GlycerPump produces a pump that is categorically superior to anything at half the citrulline dose. Someone who wants their pre-workout and creatine in one product — the full 5g creatine dose eliminates the need for a separate creatine supplement. Users who dislike beta-alanine tingling — there is none here.
❌Look elsewhere if: You are a drug-tested athlete — no formal banned substance certification. You need beta-alanine for muscular endurance performance (supplement it separately if using Gorilla Mode). You avoid artificial sweeteners. You train in the evening — 400mg caffeine will significantly impact sleep.
