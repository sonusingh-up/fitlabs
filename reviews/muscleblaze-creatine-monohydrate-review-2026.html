What Is MuscleBlaze CreAMP Creatine?

MuscleBlaze is India's largest homegrown sports nutrition brand — founded in 2012 under Bright Lifecare Pvt. Ltd., headquartered in Gurugram, Haryana, manufactured in Himachal Pradesh. CreAMP represents their most advanced creatine formula: pharmaceutical-grade micronized creatine monohydrate combined with CreAbsorb™, a patented absorption enhancement blend. It is the only Indian creatine with both a published peer-reviewed bioavailability RCT and dual Labdoor USA certification (accuracy and purity).

📚 The Published Clinical Evidence: Trehan et al. (2025) published a double-blind, randomized, two-arm crossover oral bioavailability study in Cureus (PMC11966180) comparing MuscleBlaze CreAMP™ vs standard micronized creatine in 32 healthy males aged 18–50 under fasting conditions. The study found statistically significant improvement in creatine bioavailability for CreAMP™, attributed to CreAbsorb™ reducing creatine degradation to creatinine during GI transit. This is the only published clinical trial for any Indian creatine brand — a meaningful differentiator.

CreAbsorb™ Technology — The Science

CreAbsorb™ (Indian Patent IN202311057466) is a proprietary 0.1g blend added to each serving alongside the 3g of creatine monohydrate. The mechanism: creatine is inherently unstable in acidic environments (stomach pH 1.5–3.5) and can spontaneously cyclise into creatinine — the inactive breakdown product that passes through without benefit. CreAbsorb™ is formulated to stabilise creatine during gastric transit, reducing this conversion and increasing the amount of intact creatine that reaches the small intestine for absorption.

The 2025 Cureus RCT demonstrated this effect in a controlled setting, with statistically significant improvements in serum creatine bioavailability. The study was conducted and funded by Bright Lifecare (MuscleBlaze's parent), which is standard industry practice for ingredient validation studies but means independent replication would further strengthen the claims. The mechanism itself — reducing creatinine conversion — is scientifically valid and well-established.

The 3g Dose — Honest Assessment

The ISSN Position Stand recommends 3–5g/day as the maintenance dose for creatine, with 5g being the most commonly studied and recommended single daily dose. MuscleBlaze's 3g scoop sits at the lower end of this range. For casual gym-goers training 3–4x/week, 3g/day will still produce meaningful phosphocreatine saturation over 4–5 weeks. For serious athletes prioritising maximum saturation, we recommend 1.5 scoops (4.5g) daily — which is practical given the 250g and 500g tub options.

Labdoor Certification — Why It Matters for Indian Buyers

Supplement quality control in India has historically been inconsistent, with labelling inaccuracies and adulteration concerns documented across the market. MuscleBlaze's Labdoor USA certification independently verifies: (1) that the 3g of creatine stated on the label is actually present in each serving, and (2) that no significant heavy metal contamination or impurity issues were detected. For Indian consumers who cannot easily access NSF or Informed Sport testing, Labdoor certification provides the most meaningful available assurance of what's actually in the product.
