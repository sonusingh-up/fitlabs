What Is BULK Black — And Why Does It Exist?

Transparent Labs BULK Black is the high-stimulant version of the brand's flagship BULK pre-workout — designed for experienced users who want clinical doses of every major pre-workout category (pumps, endurance, energy, focus, testosterone support) in a single, fully transparent formula. It is not a "more caffeine" reformulation of BULK. It is a categorically more ambitious product: more citrulline, more caffeine, higher-dose nootropics, added B vitamins and vitamin D, and the same zero-proprietary-blend commitment that made the original BULK famous.

The pre-workout market is dominated by products that hide inadequate ingredient doses inside proprietary blends — a practice that lets brands list impressive ingredients at a fraction of the clinical dose needed to produce any measurable effect. BULK Black's defining feature is that it refuses to do this. Every ingredient, every dose, on the label. This is verifiable against the clinical literature. It is also why this review is longer than most — because when a formula actually discloses everything, there is actually something worth analysing.

I tested BULK Black across 6 weeks of resistance training (4 sessions/week, upper/lower split) purchasing three tubs personally without any brand relationship. This review reflects those results honestly.

🔬 The transparency standard: Transparent Labs holds Informed Choice certification — the voluntary programme where supplements are randomly purchased from retail shelves and tested monthly for banned substances and label accuracy. This isn't a one-time test on a hand-selected batch; it's ongoing verification. For competitive athletes and anyone serious about what they consume, this certification is the minimum acceptable standard in 2026. BULK Black has maintained this certification since launch.

Ingredient Deep-Dive — The Science Behind Each Dose

Citrulline Malate 2:1 — The Pump Engine
8,000mg
Very Strong Evidence Clinical Dose ✔ IOC Category A

L-Citrulline Malate (the citrulline salt of malic acid) is the most evidence-supported nitric oxide precursor in sports nutrition. Citrulline converts to arginine in the kidneys, which then drives nitric oxide synthase (NOS) to produce NO — a vasodilator that widens blood vessels and increases blood flow to working muscles. Unlike L-arginine supplementation (which is poorly absorbed orally), citrulline malate reliably raises plasma arginine and NO levels because it bypasses first-pass liver metabolism. The 2:1 ratio means approximately 5,300mg of actual citrulline per 8,000mg dose — comfortably above the 3,000–6,000mg threshold validated in RCTs for pump, endurance, and lactate clearance effects. A landmark study (Pérez-Guisado & Jakeman, 2010) demonstrated 8g of citrulline malate significantly reduced muscle soreness and increased repetitions to failure vs placebo in a bench press protocol. This is that exact dose.

Beta-Alanine — Endurance & Carnosine Loading
4,000mg
Very Strong Evidence Clinical Dose ✔ Paresthesia — normal side effect

Beta-alanine is a beta-amino acid that combines with L-histidine to form carnosine in skeletal muscle. Carnosine buffers the hydrogen ions (H+) produced during high-intensity exercise — the ions responsible for the "burn" and the drop in force production during near-failure sets. Supplementing with beta-alanine chronically (4–6 weeks) saturates muscle carnosine stores, extending the time before acid-mediated fatigue limits performance. A 2012 meta-analysis in Amino Acids (Hobson et al.) analysing 15 studies confirmed statistically significant improvements in exercise capacity at doses of 3.2g/day or more. At 4,000mg per serving, BULK Black exceeds the minimum clinical threshold and matches the upper efficacy range for acute dosing. The paresthesia (skin tingling, particularly on face, ears, and forearms) that many users experience is a harmless pharmacological effect from beta-alanine's interaction with sensory receptors — not an allergic reaction or sign of toxicity. BetaPure® Betaine Anhydrous — Power & Cellular Hydration
2,500mg
Strong Evidence Exact Clinical Dose ✔

Betaine (trimethylglycine or TMG) is a naturally occurring compound found in sugar beets that serves as an osmolyte — helping cells retain water and maintain volume under osmotic stress. This cellular hydration effect is important for maintaining muscle performance during training. Multiple studies have shown betaine supplementation at 2,500mg/day improves power output, work capacity, and body composition. A 2011 study in the Journal of Strength & Conditioning Research (Trepanowski et al.) demonstrated significant improvements in bench press and squat performance with chronic betaine supplementation. BetaPure® is the pharmaceutical-grade form with standardised purity — not generic betaine anhydrous, which can have variable quality and bioavailability. The exact 2,500mg dose here matches the most consistently replicated human trial dosing.

AlphaSize® Alpha-GPC — The Premium Nootropic
300mg
Strong Evidence Best Bioavailability Growth Hormone Support

Alpha-GPC (Alpha-Glyceryl Phosphoryl Choline) is the most bioavailable oral choline source available. Choline is an essential precursor to acetylcholine — the primary neurotransmitter mediating neuromuscular junction signalling. In the gym context, higher acetylcholine availability enhances the mind-muscle connection, sharpens focus, and improves motor unit recruitment. A 2008 study (Ziegenfuss et al.) demonstrated Alpha-GPC supplementation at 600mg acutely increased growth hormone secretion and produced meaningful improvements in peak power output in trained athletes. AlphaSize® is the trademarked, standardised form — 50% active alpha-GPC by weight, meaning 300mg of AlphaSize delivers 150mg of actual alpha-GPC. This is the most common effective dose in acute performance studies. The nootropic and focus effects are among the most consistently reported user experiences with BULK Black.

Caffeine Anhydrous (275mg) + Infinergy® DiCaffeine Malate (30mg)
305mg total
Very Strong Evidence IOC Category A Dual-Phase Delivery

BULK Black uses a dual-caffeine strategy: 275mg of standard caffeine anhydrous (fast-acting, rapid CNS stimulation) plus 30mg of Infinergy® DiCaffeine Malate — a buffered form of caffeine bonded to malic acid that releases more slowly in the gut, extending the energy duration and reducing the drop-off that follows a caffeine anhydrous peak. The combined 305mg total caffeine places BULK Black in the high-stim category — equivalent to approximately 3.5 strong espresso shots. At 3–6mg/kg, this is appropriate for athletes weighing 50–100kg. For context: the EFSA identifies 400mg/day as the safe upper limit for healthy adults; one scoop of BULK Black uses ~75% of this daily limit. The L-Theanine at 200mg is critically important here — it modulates caffeine's effects by reducing the jitteriness and anxiety that can accompany high-dose caffeine without blunting the alertness or energy benefits. The caffeine-theanine combination is the most well-replicated cognitive enhancement stack in the literature. Full caffeine science guide →

L-Tyrosine — Stress Resilience & Focus
1,000mg
Strong Evidence Especially effective under stress/fatigue

L-Tyrosine is a conditionally essential amino acid that serves as the biosynthetic precursor to dopamine, norepinephrine, and epinephrine. During intense exercise, catecholamine neurotransmitters are depleted — contributing to both central fatigue (reduced drive) and peripheral fatigue. Tyrosine supplementation replenishes this precursor pool, helping maintain neurotransmitter synthesis during training stress. Research demonstrates tyrosine is particularly effective under conditions of stress, sleep deprivation, and cognitive demand — precisely the context of a hard training session. At 1,000mg, BULK Black is at the lower-to-mid end of the evidence-based range (500–2,000mg) but delivers meaningful support. The synergy with Alpha-GPC — which increases acetylcholine via the cholinergic pathway while tyrosine supports the catecholaminergic pathway — creates complementary nootropic coverage across multiple neurotransmitter systems.

Astragin® — The Absorption Multiplier
50mg
Exact Clinical Dose ✔ Enhances all active ingredients

Astragin® is a patented extract of Astragalus membranaceus and Panax notoginseng that upregulates intestinal transport proteins (particularly mTOR-regulated tight junction proteins) to improve nutrient absorption across the gut wall. The mechanism is well-documented: Astragin increases the expression of transporters that citrulline, arginine, amino acids, and other actives use to cross the intestinal epithelium. At 50mg — the exact clinical dose — it meaningfully improves the bioavailability of the entire BULK Black formula. Its inclusion represents a level of formulation sophistication absent from most competitors: not just adding ingredients, but ensuring those ingredients are actually absorbed effectively.

Boron Citrate + Vitamin D3 — Testosterone Support
3mg + 75mcg (3,000 IU)
Vitamin D: Strong Evidence Boron: Moderate Evidence

Boron (3mg) inhibits sex hormone-binding globulin (SHBG) — the protein that binds and inactivates free testosterone. By reducing SHBG, boron increases free testosterone availability. A 2011 study demonstrated 10mg/day boron produced significant increases in free testosterone over 7 days — at 3mg, the effect is smaller but meaningful when consistent. Vitamin D3 at 75mcg (3,000 IU) supports testosterone production through the androgen receptor pathway; low vitamin D status is strongly associated with reduced testosterone in men across multiple studies. For an athlete already supplementing vitamin D, this adds to the daily total; for those who are vitamin D-deficient (common in indoor athletes and those in low-sun climates), this is a meaningful performance-relevant addition.

6 Weeks of Real-World Testing — Honest Report

I tested BULK Black Black Cherry flavour across a 6-week upper/lower resistance training programme (4 sessions/week), taken consistently 25–30 minutes pre-workout on an empty stomach or 2 hours post-meal.

Energy & Stimulant Profile

The 305mg dual-caffeine delivery is the best I've experienced in a pre-workout. The caffeine anhydrous produces onset within 20 minutes — alertness sharpens, perceived effort drops, motivational drive increases. The Infinergy® then extends this plateau meaningfully. I did not experience the energy cliff around the 90-minute mark that is characteristic of single-source caffeine pre-workouts. Sessions lasting 70–80 minutes maintained consistent energy throughout without any crash following. The 200mg L-Theanine addition is the critical variable here — without it, 305mg of caffeine would produce significant jitteriness in many users. With it, the stimulation is clean and directed rather than anxious and scattered.

Pump & Vascularity

The 8,000mg citrulline malate produced the most consistent pump effect I've experienced from a pre-workout. Within 35–40 minutes of ingestion, vasodilation in the forearms, biceps, and deltoids was visibly and tactilely significant during upper body sessions. The pump persisted well into the post-workout window. On leg days, the citrulline's contribution to quadriceps and hamstring blood flow was notable in terms of both the pump sensation and perceived muscle fullness during the session. The betaine contribution becomes more apparent with consistent use (weeks 3–6) as cellular hydration status compounds.

Focus & Mind-Muscle Connection

The Alpha-GPC + Tyrosine + Theanine nootropic stack is where BULK Black most clearly separates from competitors. The focus during training was notably different from caffeine-only products — there is a quality of attention that makes each set feel more deliberate and controlled. The mind-muscle connection on isolation movements (particularly cable work and machine exercises requiring precise motor patterns) was the clearest practical benefit. This is acetylcholine-mediated — it's the Alpha-GPC doing its job.

Beta-Alanine Paresthesia

At 4,000mg, the beta-alanine tingling is significant. During the first two weeks the paresthesia was pronounced — particularly on the scalp, ears, forearms, and face — lasting 20–35 minutes. By week four it had reduced substantially as carnosine loading approached saturation. I classify this as expected and acceptable, but it is worth clearly warning new users: this is normal, harmless, and temporary, but it is intense at this dose. For users who find paresthesia intolerable, starting with a half scoop for 2–3 weeks before progressing to a full scoop is the sensible protocol.

⏱ The optimal timing and dosing protocol we used: Full scoop mixed with 400–500ml of cold water, taken 25–30 minutes before training on an empty stomach or 2+ hours after eating. The larger water volume (vs the 240ml suggested on the label) noticeably reduces the beta-alanine intensity and improves the flavour profile without affecting ingredient efficacy. Never take 2 scoops — the 610mg total caffeine would exceed safe limits for most adults. If caffeine-sensitive, start at half-scoop to assess tolerance.

Who Should Buy BULK Black — And Who Shouldn't

✅ Buy BULK Black if you are:

  • Experienced lifters who have built caffeine tolerance — 305mg is a serious stimulant load. If you're already consuming 200–300mg caffeine daily from coffee and feel little effect, BULK Black will work well. If not, start at half scoop.
  • Athletes who train in the morning or early afternoon — the 305mg caffeine and dual-phase delivery means effects persist for 4–6 hours. Evening training (after 5pm) risks sleep interference.
  • Anyone frustrated by underdosed pre-workouts — if you've used pre-workouts that "do nothing," BULK Black's clinical doses across every major category will produce noticeable effects you can actually attribute to the formula.
  • Drug-tested athletes — Informed Choice certification is monthly blind-sample tested. This is the gold standard for banned substance assurance.
  • People who want no artificial ingredients — stevia-sweetened, no artificial colours, no sucralose, no Ace-K. One of the cleanest high-stim formulas available.
  • Strength athletes and bodybuilders targeting PR sessions — the citrulline + betaine + beta-alanine stack specifically targets the mechanisms most relevant to maximal strength and high-rep endurance.

❌ Look elsewhere if you are:

  • Caffeine-sensitive or new to pre-workouts — 305mg total caffeine will cause anxiety, rapid heart rate, and jitteriness in people unaccustomed to high-dose stimulants. Start with the regular BULK (200mg caffeine) first.
  • Evening trainers — 305mg with dual-phase delivery will significantly affect sleep if taken after 3–4pm for most people.
  • Budget buyers — $59.99 for 30 servings is $2.00/serving. If budget is the primary concern, Myprotein's pre-workouts or C4 Original offer decent but underdosed alternatives at 50–60% of this cost.
  • People who dislike beta-alanine tingling — at 4g, the paresthesia is intense, especially in the first 2–3 weeks. If you cannot tolerate it, choose a BA-free pre-workout.
  • People who need creatine in their pre-workout — BULK Black has none. Transparent Labs specifically recommends post-workout creatine timing (correctly, per current evidence) and sells a standalone creatine + HMB product for this purpose.
